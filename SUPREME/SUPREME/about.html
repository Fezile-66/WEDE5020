About Us

History
The brand was founded in 1994 by American-British businessman and fashion designer James Jebbia. During the formation of the brand, Jebbia was inspired by a book on Barbara Kruger's artwork, which influenced the design of Supreme's red box logo with a white Futura Heavy Oblique font.

Vision and mission

Supreme does not publish a standard business mission statement, but its operational strategy is based on authenticity and exclusivity. The brand's basic principle is "being cool enough to survive," with a distinct identity that prioritises the subculture of skateboarding, art, and music over mass-market appeal.
